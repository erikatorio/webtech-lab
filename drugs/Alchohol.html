What is Alchohol?

  • A legal, sedative drug which changes the way we feel.
  • Pure alcohol is a colourless, odourless and inflammable fluid.
  • Alcohol as a drug does not contain any nutrients for the body.
  • Alcoholic beverages have been used in many societies for many purposes.

Bakakeng Norte cannot control the use of Alchohol, they are still needed to be helped. So now what are programs Bakakeng Norte has?

Seminars

Bakakeng Norte makes use of General Assemblies and Seminars for Youths. They invite guess speakers for sharing and giving out advices.

With the General Assembly they also ask Religous Sects to ask and convinced those who have been users to direct their time to other activites such as ball games and other activites that Bakakeng Norte/Sur gives out.

Business Establishments

Bakakeng Norte make sure that they inform Business Establishments and advice them not to sell liquor to minors. Strict implementation of their closing time "10 pm" to prevent any commotion.
