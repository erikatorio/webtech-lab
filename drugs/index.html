Substance Abuse and Rehabilitation

Substance abuse prevention is also known as "Drug Prevention". It is a process that attemps to prevent the onset of substance use or limit the development of problems associated with using psychoactive's substance.

Most drugs can alter a person's thinking and judgement, leading to health risks, including addiction, drugged driving and infectious diseas.

People drink to socialize,celebrate and relax. Alcohol often has strong effect on people- throughout history, people have struggled to understand and manage alcohol's power.
