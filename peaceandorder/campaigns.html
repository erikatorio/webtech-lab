Bakakeng Norte: Maintaining the Peace and Order

Strict and Stress Free Environment

Bakakeng Norte makes sure that the environment that the people live in is calm and quiet as much as possible. It is a rule for them to be friendly with everyone. Violence is never tolerated. Any form of violence is responded to immediately and the barangay officials think of a way to remedy the situation.

Religious Rehabilitation

Once a member of their society is troubled, the barangay offers religious activities that can help them be distracted from their current situation.

CCTV

The Bakakeng Norte officials have installed CCTVs around the perimeters for monitoring any unusual activity. The CCTVs can also help in referencing any indicidents that may occur around the barangay.

Barangay Discipline

The barangay strictly enforces the curfew for minors at 10 pm. Other than that, barangay tanods are always patrolling the barangay. The barangay officials also asked the neighborhood to be on "Neighborhood Watch" wherein the neighborhood is asked to be aware and be on the lookout for each other.
